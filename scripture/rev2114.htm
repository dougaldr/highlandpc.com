1 Then I saw a new heaven and a new earth, for the first heaven and the first earth had passed away, and there was no longer any  sea.  2 I saw the Holy City, the new Jerusalem, coming down out of heaven from God, prepared as a bride beautifully dressed for her husband.  3 And I heard a loud voice from the throne saying , "Now the dwelling of God is in them.  They will be his people, and God himself will be with them and be their God.  4 He will wipe every tear from their eyes.  There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain, for the old order of things has passed away."
