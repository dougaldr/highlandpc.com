5 Your love, O LORD, reaches to the heavens, your faithfulness to the skies.  6 Your righteousness is like the mighty mountains, your justice like the great deep.  O LORD, you preserve both man and beast.  7 How priceless is your unfailing love! Both high and low among men find refuge in the shadow of your wings.  8 They feast on the abundance of your house; you give them drink form your river of delights.  9 For with you is the fountain of life; in your light we see light.  10 Continue your love to those who know you, your righteousness to the upright in heart.
