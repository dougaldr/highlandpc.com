16 For everything in the world—the cravings of sinful man, the lust of his eyes and the boasting of what he has and does—comes not from the Father but from the world.  17 The world and its desires pass away, but the man who does the will of God lives forever.  18 Dear children, this is the last hour; and as you have heard that the antichrist is coming, even now many antichrists have come.  This is ho we know it is the last hour.
